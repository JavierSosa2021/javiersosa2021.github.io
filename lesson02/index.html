Site Name

Sunny Days News Santiago

Domain

www.sunnydaynewssantiago.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a kind of 'one stop information stop' for the residents of Southeast Santiago. Specifically the residents of Estacion Central, Los Cerrillos, and Maipu. Information will include current weather, relevant local news, and promote events. Up to date weather forcasts will be kept current in an easy to use format. News will be updated regularly with relevant local data. Events advertised will be specific to the local community.
